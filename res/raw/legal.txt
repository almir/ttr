Application behaves as an airplane or silent mode scheduler,&nbsp;
enabling you to schedule time when you want your device to&nbsp;
automatically enable or disable airplane or silent mode.<br>
You can choose whether you want your schedule to execute only&nbsp;
once or on specific days of week.<br>
Once it's set just enable it and let the application do its thing.<br><br>
