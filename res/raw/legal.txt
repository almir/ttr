Application behaves as an airplane or silent mode scheduler, 
enabling you to schedule time when you want your device to 
automatically enable or disable airplane or silent mode.<br>
You can choose whether you want your schedule to execute only 
once or on specific days of week.<br>
Once it's set just enable it and let the application do its thing.<br><br>
